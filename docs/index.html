About me

Andrew is 30 years old and is based in London, UK. Graduating from the University of Birmingham in 2015 with a First Class Honours Degree in Mathematics and Music with a Year in Computer Science, he joined Lloyds Banking Group on their Retail graduate programme.

Andrew has a wide range of experience in financial services, from customer facing roles through to head office roles over the past seven years. Following a four-year period as an Executive Assistant to the Retail Bank Chief Operating Officer, he has gained two years of experience in data, both in strategy roles and most recently heading up the Retail federated data science team. Andrew currently leads a team of 10 Data Scientists and Machine Learning Engineers and by the end of 2023 will be responsible for a portfolio of around 10 ML models used across the Retail division of the Group.

Outside of work, Andrew is a football enthusiast and is an avid supporter of Liverpool Football Club. He is also a keen classical guitarist and enjoys travelling and exploring Europe.
